MealTracker Privacy Policy

The meal tracking app designed for Australian paramedics

Last updated: 30/01/2026

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information only when you submit a support request:

User‑Provided Data

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Message content
  • Steps to reproduce (optional)
  • Screenshot (optional)

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version

This information is collected solely to help diagnose and resolve support issues.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only for support purposes, including:

  • Understanding your issue
  • Reproducing bugs
  • Improving app stability and performance
  • Responding to your support request

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, tracking, or profiling.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

On Your Device

Support requests are temporarily stored locally using Hive, an encrypted key‑value database.

On Our Servers

  • The data is sent to our Cloudflare Worker
  • The Worker forwards the request to our email provider (Resend)
  • The Worker does not permanently store your data
  • No databases or logs store your personal information beyond what Cloudflare logs automatically for security

4. How Your Data Is Transmitted

All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS with TLS encryption.

Screenshots and attachments are also transmitted securely.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data (pending submissions) is stored until successfully sent
  • Support emails are stored in our email inbox until manually deleted
  • No long‑term server‑side storage exists beyond email delivery

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Resend (email delivery provider)
  • Cloudflare (infrastructure provider)

Both providers process your data only to deliver your support request.

7. Your Rights

You may request:

  • A copy of your submitted support data
  • Deletion of your support data
  • Correction of inaccurate information

MealTracker is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
